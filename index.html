Warped Lines

A 3D map of income equality on the New York City subway. The "height" of the subway lines correspond to the median household income of the surrounding area. Select a line to see more details:

Drag cursor on map to rotate. Pinch or scroll to zoom. Right-click or use two fingers to pan.

Select a station below to zoom view it on the map.

Created by Brian Foo using Median household income by census tract, 2017 ACS 5-year estimates (B19013) and MTA subway station data. Code is open source.
